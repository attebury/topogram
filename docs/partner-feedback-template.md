# Partner Feedback Template

Use this note after a serious alpha conversation, async review, or live walkthrough.

The goal is not to capture everything. The goal is to capture the few signals that matter for deciding:

- does the current wedge resonate?
- is this team a real alpha fit?
- what is confusing, missing, or off-strategy?
